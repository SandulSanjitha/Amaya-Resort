World Best Facilities for You

Swimming Pools

Plunge into the relaxed atmosphere that floats around not one, but two of our swimming pools. Feel all your fatigue wash away and a sense of calm take over your senses as you swim a few laps.Our rooftop pool, located at the amaya Spa complex has a more therapeutic vibe. Dive into it, do a few strokes till you feel your appetite building, head to the Jacuzzi to relax, and then finally dig into a healthy snack at The Café.

Gymnasiums

Why should you miss out on fitness when you’re travelling? Our two gymnasiums ensure that your busy routine doesn’t break your health routine. Book a slot and we’ll help you work out the right routine for your wellbeing.Make the most of our state-of-the-art facilities and equipments. Our steam room, sauna and lockers make the experience comfortable for you.

Boardrooms

Set a milestone at a landmark. Once you’re with us, you won’t look back. Choose to host an international conference or launch your newest product in our stellar banquet spaces. Sign off the biggest deals in our boardrooms equipped with state of the art technology and reach for the stars. Perfect spaces for meetings conferences cocktail receptions seminars and workshops.

Pub

A traditional British pub, with wooden interiors, bar tables placed a little away from dinner tables, and a high intensity match being screened live – imagine the atmosphere! It’s the only place where friends get divided into teams and still drink to it.Whether your favourite team wins or not, you’ll surely score the highest, cheering for them with us

Spa

An intuitive art of touch and high standards of dedicated service are the highlights of each Amaya Spa experience. Each Amaya Spa therapist undergoes at least 350 hours of training based on stringent curriculum of the accredited Sri lankan Spa Academy.
